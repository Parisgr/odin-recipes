ALFREDO CREME DE LA CREME

Step into a world of creamy indulgence with our"Alfredo Creme De La Creme Pasta" recipe. This culinary masterpiece combines succulent shrimp, vibrant bell peppers, and savory onions, all enveloped in a luscious and velvety Alfredo sauce.

With every forkful, you'll experience the perfect harmony of flavors, from the richness of Parmesan cheese to the delicate balance of garlic and black pepper. Whether you're seeking a comforting weeknight dinner or aiming to impress guests with a luxurious feast, this dish will take your taste buds on a delectable journey.

So, get ready to delight your senses and savor the irresistible allure of our velvety shrimp Alfredo pasta. Let the creamy symphony of flavors whisk you away into a world of culinary delight!

Ingredients

  • 20 medium-sized shrimps, peeled and deveined
  • 1 large bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 and 1/2 cups cooking cream
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 8 ounces fettuccine or pasta of your choice

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set aside.
  2. Prepare the shrimp: Season the shrimp with salt, black pepper, and garlic powder. In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the seasoned shrimp and cook for about 2-3 minutes per side until they turn pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Sauté the vegetables: In the same skillet, add a little more olive oil if needed. Add the sliced bell pepper, chopped onion, and minced garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are tender and slightly caramelized.
  4. Prepare the Alfredo sauce: Pour the chicken broth into the skillet with the sautéed vegetables. Stir in the cooking cream and bring the mixture to a simmer. Let it cook for a few minutes until the sauce thickens slightly. Then, stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until it melts and combines with the sauce.
  5. Combine the pasta and sauce: Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the Alfredo sauce. Toss everything together until the pasta is coated evenly with the sauce. If the sauce is too thick, you can add a little more chicken broth or cooking cream to reach the desired consistency.
  6. Serve: Place the shrimp on top of the Alfredo pasta and garnish with some extra Parmesan cheese if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!

This recipe should yield a delicious and creamy Alfredo pasta with shrimp, bell peppers, and onions. Feel free to adjust the seasoning according to your taste preferences.
