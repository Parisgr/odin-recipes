Fresh and Festive Mediterranean Delight Salad

Welcome to the world of vibrant flavors and refreshing ingredients with our "Fresh and Festive Mediterranean Delight Salad" recipe. This salad is a celebration of Mediterranean-inspired goodness, combining a colorful array of fresh vegetables, tangy feta cheese, and aromatic herbs.

With each bite, you'll be transported to the sun-kissed shores of the Mediterranean, where the flavors dance on your palate and bring a smile to your face. Whether you're looking for a light lunch, a side dish for a barbecue, or a vibrant addition to your dinner table, this salad is sure to delight your taste buds and brighten up any meal.

So, let's dive into this refreshing culinary adventure and savor the vibrant flavors of the Mediterranean!

Ingredients

  • Mixed salad greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the extra virgin olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, salt, and black pepper until well combined. Set aside.
  2. In a large salad bowl, combine the mixed salad greens, cherry tomatoes, diced cucumber, sliced red onion, crumbled feta cheese, sliced black olives, chopped fresh basil, and chopped fresh parsley.
  3. Drizzle the dressing over the salad ingredients. Toss gently to coat everything evenly.
  4. Serve the salad immediately and enjoy!

This salad recipe offers a combination of fresh vegetables, tangy feta cheese, and aromatic herbs. The dressing adds a zesty and tangy flavor to the salad. Feel free to customize the salad by adding other ingredients like grilled chicken, avocado, or toasted nuts to suit your taste preferences. Enjoy this delicious and vibrant salad!
